Achievements:
    * Unlocked by doing little side quests that don't necessarily help reach the end goal
    * Unable to see Achievements until after finishing the game

Stat Tracking:
    * Time/moves/turns spent in each room

Variable Speed Rooms:
    * Time moves faster/slower in certain rooms

Coin Flip Room:
    * Coin in room heads initially
    * If the coin is flipped and is tails, one of the exits leads to a hidden room
    * If the coin is flipped and is heads, the exit resets to it's normal path
